Category

Category management is a system used to group and organize products, services, or data into specific segments for easier management, access, and analysis. This structure is essential for businesses that handle a large inventory or dataset, allowing users to locate, filter, and manage content efficiently.


     

Customer

Customer Management is the process of handling customer interactions and data throughout the customer lifecycle, aiming to improve customer satisfaction, loyalty, and long-term business success. In a web-based application, this typically includes adding, viewing, editing, and deleting customer records.


     

Employee

The Employees Management section provides essential tools to manage company staff information efficiently. This module is designed for human resource managers, administrators, and company owners who need a reliable way to track employee details, manage updates, and ensure data consistency.


     

Order

The Order Management system is a core feature that allows users to efficiently handle customer orders within the application. This system includes functionality for adding, editing, viewing, searching, and deleting orders — all with seamless integration using Bootstrap 5 and JavaScript with persistent storage via localStorage.


Order Detail

Order Details represent the specific information about items within an order. While an Order tracks the overall transaction (such as customer info and date), the Order Details focus on each product included in the order — quantity, unit price, discount, and total per item.


Product

Our product line is designed to meet the diverse needs of our customers, offering a wide selection of high-quality goods that combine functionality, style, and value. Each product is carefully curated to ensure it meets our strict standards for quality, performance, and customer satisfaction.


Shipper

The Shipper Management page allows users to manage all shipping companies or delivery personnel involved in transporting products to customers. This module is essential for maintaining accurate delivery records and improving logistic efficiency.


Supplier

Supplier Management is a critical function in any business that deals with inventory, manufacturing, or service delivery. It involves maintaining accurate records of companies or individuals who provide the goods and services necessary for business operations. Effective supplier management ensures timely procurement, cost control, and high-quality inputs.
